We are all in this together. And successful cooperation requires effective communication. This series of Public Service Announcements has been created to help all Do it Best retailers provide their customers with the latest updates and information. Recorded in both English and Spanish, these PSA’s are totally free. Just select and download the messages that are appropriate for your community’s guidelines and your store’s current services.

We are on your team

Since 2003, Do it Best Radio has provided in-store music and messaging to Do it Best stores nationwide. Designed to enhance the shopping experience, the music is upbeat and the advertising promotes products available in the store. An audio stream is delivered via the internet to each individual store so messaging can be store-specific. Maintaining a positive atmosphere is still important. But during this challenging season, we want to use our skills to provide maximum value to the entire Do it Best family.

Your message for your community

The Do it Best Radio team is eager to help your business as the world continues to adjust during the current health situation. We appreciate the effort required for your essential business to meet the needs of a community and adhere to frequently changing guidelines. With frontline input from our subscribers, we have produced 15 public service announcements that address a range of topics from social distancing to procedural changes. Since each area of the country is moving toward recovery at a different pace, select the messages that are appropriate for your store.

How you can use it

We encourage you to use these messages on your in-store radio, music on hold, or social media. The PSA's are available for immediate download from the links below. They are absolutely free - they are yours to use as you see fit . We hope that these announcements will assist you in communicating effectively to customers, employees, and your community. Don't hesitate to contact us if you have any challenges we might be able to help you through.
